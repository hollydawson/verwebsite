Who We Are

We are a group of students from the Australian National University, currently in the final year of our Bachelor of Engineering (Honours). As a team we are working on Project Ver as our Capstone project which is due to conclude in October, 2024.

Our team brings together a diverse range of skills, including machine learning, hardware design, and software development. Each member plays a critical role in driving the project forward, from conceptualisation to implementation. Our collective goal is to push the boundaries of what assistive technology can achieve, ultimately making a meaningful difference in people’s lives.

If you’re interested in learning more about our work or collaborating with us we would love to hear from you. Please feel free to reach out!
